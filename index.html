Luffy

Main character of the anime 'One Piece' and Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, Monkey D. Luffy - is on an adventure with his crew on The Thousand Sunny to sail around the Grand Line, find the one piece, and become The Pirate King.

    Here are some fun facts about Luffy:
  • Ate the Gum-Gum devil fruit when he was a child, turning him into a rubber man
  • He currently has a bounty of 1,500,000,000 berries
  • He is one of the 'Worst Generation'
  • Unofficially known as The Fifth Emperor of the Sea
  • Sailed out to sea at 17
